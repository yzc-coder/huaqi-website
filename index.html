About Shandong Huaqi

Your Trusted Global Vehicle Supplier

Shandong Huaqi International Trade Co., Ltd. is located in Liangshan County, Shandong Province, China, the largest trailer production base in China. With nearly 20 years of industry experience, we are a professional enterprise integrating R&D, production and sales.

We have passed ISO9001-2000 certification and 3C certification, and are a national "Announcement" management enterprise. We provide one-stop solutions for global customers, from semi-trailers, trucks to special vehicles and engineering machinery.

Our products are exported to more than 50 countries around the world, with complete after-sales service system, to ensure your worry-free use.
